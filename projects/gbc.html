Online Magazine for iGaming & Crypto

Project Overview:

GBC Time is a full-featured online magazine and news portal focused on the iGaming industry, covering topics such as gambling, affiliate marketing, crypto, betting, payments, regulation, and interviews/case studies. My role involved designing, developing, and deploying the website from end to end, ensuring it is content-rich, performant, and scalable.

Key Features & Functionality:

1. Robust Content Architecture A multi-category structure supports sections like News & Press Releases, Casino, Affiliate Marketing, Crypto, Betting, Payments, Directory, Interviews & Case Studies, and Ratings. Featured posts, article teasers, directory listings, and rating pages provide dynamic content presentation.
2. Editorial & Interview Workflow The Interviews & Case Studies section is structured to showcase Q&A style content, with rich media (images) and authoring flexibility. Content previews, pagination, and archive navigation make it easy for readers to browse back catalog content.
3. Ratings & Directory Modules A “Ratings” section with curated rankings across subdomains (e.g. top payment providers, affiliate networks) allows editorial control over comparative content. A “Directory” component supports listing of companies, aggregators, service providers, and more.
4. Responsive & Visual Design Visually rich layouts include banners, featured images, and promotional graphics. Fully responsive design ensures readability and usability across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
5. Contact & Social Integration The Contact page includes email, Telegram handle, and social links. Integration with social media (e.g. Telegram, LinkedIn) for community engagement.

Technical Approach & Challenges

CMS / Backend: Built on a content management system that supports custom content types (articles, interviews, directories, ratings) and dynamic listing.
Performance & SEO: Optimized for fast page loads (lazy loading images, caching strategies) and SEO (clean URLs, metadata, structured HTML).
Scalability & Maintainability: Modular templates and component-based architecture to handle growth in content volume and new feature expansions.
Media Handling: Efficient image handling (thumbnails, adaptive resizing) for featured posts and embedded media.
UX & Navigation: Clear menu hierarchies, “featured posts” sections, infinite scroll / pagination, and filtering by category to facilitate discovery.

Impact & Value Delivered

Positioned GBC Time as a professional, authoritative voice in the iGaming and crypto space.
Enhanced readability and engagement by structuring content cleanly and visually.
Provided editors with tools to manage content easily — adding articles, interviews, ratings, directory entries — without needing heavy custom dev work each time.
Laid out a scalable foundation so future features (e.g. user account system, subscription paywall, more interactive ratings) can be added without major rework.
